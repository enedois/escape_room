Pirate Room

You and your fellow pirates have been captured by the Royal Navy and imprisoned on a ship bound for England. You must escape before the ship reaches port, or you'll be hanged as pirates! You have one hour to escape the ship before it's too late.

You'll need to use all your pirate skills to find a way out of your cell, and then navigate your way through the ship to the escape boats. Be careful though, the ship is guarded by the Royal Navy and they'll be watching your every move.

If you can escape the ship and make it to the escape boats, you'll be free to sail the seas again as a pirate! But if you're caught, you'll be hanged as a pirate.
